What is a Gourmet Club?

For many years, Denmark has been in the absolute world elite in gourmet restaurants.
The many restaurants awarded with 2 and 3 stars respectively in the Michelin guide, have their own menus and personal touch on the infinite taste.

At Gourmet Club - Le goût infini, we create an environment that is only for the sublime palate, and we invite the world's best chefs to show their perception of perfection.

A membership of the Gourmet Club -
Le goût infini, will guarantee you a place in the most exclusive world elite of gourmet.

Let Rasmus Kofoed, the head chef from Geranium and several times honored at the Bocuse d'Or, prepare you a meal that captures Nordic gourmet art in all its glory.

Or let René Redzepi, who is the head chef at restaurant Noma on a daily basis, introduce you to The Autumn Game & Forest season.

Expect all the flavors of the forest, game, berries, mushrooms and all that Danish nature can offer.

We organize the evening, we find the premises, we provide the entertainment or the evening's theme.

You contribute with your membership, and together we chase
the infinite taste.
